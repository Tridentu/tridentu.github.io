Welcome to Tridentu Linux!

Tridentu Linux is a Linux Distro made from scratch for the terminal - all while looking good doing it.

Simple

And Effective

Tridentu provides a simple desktop/terminal setup for those who want something that works and that feels famiiar.
Bloat-free

To an extent

Anything that's optional (meaning not needed for Tridentu to be Tridentu) is packaged using Caravel - Tridentu's own package manager/format.
Beautiful

With Nord

Tridentu comes with beautiful, Nord-themed visuals - visuals that usually feel natural for the distro.
